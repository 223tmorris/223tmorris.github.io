Architect

Description

Plan, direct, or coordinate activities in such fields as architecture and engineering or research and development in these fields.

Average Salary

Median wages (2019): $69.63 hourly, $144,830 annual

Employers

  • Architectural, Engineering, and Related Services
  • Federal Executive Branch (OES Designation)
  • Scientific Research and development Services
  • Aerospace Product and Parts Manufacturing
  • Semiconductor and Other Electronic Component Manufacturing

Related Careers

  • Architects, Except Landscape and Naval: Plan and design structures such as: private residences, office buildings, theaters, factories, and other structures.
  • Civil Engineers: Do work in planning, designing, and overseeing construction and maintenance of building structures and facilities.
  • Transportation Engineers: Develop plans for transportation projects and prepare designs, specifications, or estimates for transportation facilities. Plan modificiations of existing streets, highways or freeways.
